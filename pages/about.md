## About the FAIR Metroline
The FAIR Metroline is collaborative initiative between Health-RI and the international FAIR community to provide readable and applicable guidance on how to make data FAIR.
It is based on the [Critical steps towards large-scale implementation of the FAIR data principles](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.7867293) work by Bruna dos Santos Vieira, César Henrique Bernabé, Ines Henriques, Shuxin Zhang, Alberto Ballasteros Camara, Jose Antonio Ramírez García, et al.

## AI statement
FAIR Metroline is developed with the responsible use of AI tools that assist both the writing of content and the development of the website. AI is used to assist the work of authors and developers, not to replace human judgement or accountability.

In the writing process, AI is used as a support tool. This includes brainstorming and identifying gaps in existing content, restructuring material to improve clarity and refining language at the level of grammar, spelling and consistency. AI helps make pages more readable and coherent, based on content provided by human authors.

AI tools are also used during the development of the FAIR Metroline website. This includes assisting with coding parts of the website and supporting developers in organising, refining or documenting technical components. AI is used as an aid in development work, not as an autonomous decision-maker.

Writers and developers remain fully responsible for the accuracy and quality of the content and for how the website functions. This includes checking facts, validating technical solutions and making editorial and design decisions. No AI-assisted content or code is published without human review.

## How to cite
When referencing content from the FAIR Metroline pages, please cite as follows:

> FAIR Metroline. A step-by-step route to making data FAIR (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable and Reusable). https://fairmetroline.org

The FAIR Metroline makes all its documents and data available under a [CC-BY](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/) license. For full details on licensing, please visit our License document on [GitHub](https://github.com/Health-RI/The-FAIR-Metroline/LICENSE.txt).
